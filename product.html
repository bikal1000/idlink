Simplify Identity Management

Our anonymized facial recognition authenticates patients and clinicians safely and securely. Why burden clinicians with dongles, key fobs, and pins when IDLink Systems can grant immediate access with a smart phone camera? Why worry about patient identity verification when exchanging health records? Our patented two factor authentication uses ‘Something you are’ and ‘Something you know’ to identity patients, clinicians and users.

Secure Repository

Manage access and identity using our secure cloud-based repository. Fully compliant with industry standards. OAuth and API based interfaces enable IDLink to work with hosted, on premises and cloud systems and protect information in transit and at rest.

Access Control

Reduce the risk of compromised credentials and increase user compliance and acceptance of security requirements. Our single sign-on solution grants access simply and easily without requiring external devices or codes that can be lost, stolen or abused.

Patient Identity Management

IDLink strengthens confidence in patient identity, reducing the opportunity for false positives, data breaches and fraud. Identity is based on an exact match of the anonymized facial characteristics increasing confidence and reducing manual effort to exchange and validate records.
